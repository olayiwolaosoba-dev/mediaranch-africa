Common Questions

Before you brief us.

The questions clients ask most — answered directly, because your time matters.

What's the typical turnaround for a TVC?+

From greenlight to master delivery: 3–5 weeks is standard for a 60-second TVC. Faster is possible with accelerated pre-production and pre-booked kit. We never compromise the edit to meet an impossible deadline — we'll tell you upfront if the timeline isn't realistic.

Do you shoot outside Nigeria?+

Yes — we've shot across West Africa, East Africa, Europe, and North America. For international work we typically bring core crew and pair with local fixers for gear and compliance.

Can you work with our in-house agency?+

Absolutely. A large share of our work is white-labelled for agencies and in-house teams. We operate under strict NDAs and never publicly attribute co-produced work without explicit permission.

What's your minimum engagement?+

For single projects we take briefs starting at $5k. For retainers we structure quarterly engagements. For crew-only bookings, day-rate applies.

Do you own your kit?+

Yes — we run an in-house inventory of cinema cameras, lighting, grip, audio, drones and post suites. For specialty requirements (anamorphics, underwater, techno-cranes) we partner with trusted rental houses.
